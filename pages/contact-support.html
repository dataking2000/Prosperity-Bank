Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to the most common questions about our products and services.

How do I enroll in Online and Mobile Banking?
To enroll, you will need your checking or savings account number, the last 4 digits of your Social Security number, and a valid email address. Click the 'Enroll Now' button at the top right of this page to begin the quick, secure process.
What is the Zero Liability Guarantee?
Our Zero Liability Guarantee means you are protected against unauthorized use of your credit card or debit card. If fraudulent charges occur, you will be fully reimbursed, provided you notify us promptly. Visit the Security Center for full details.
Are my deposits FDIC insured?
Yes, Prosperity Bank, N.A. is a Member FDIC. Your deposits are federally insured up to the maximum amount permitted by law (currently $250,000 per depositor, per insured bank, for each account ownership category).
How do I apply for a home loan or mortgage?
You can start your mortgage application process entirely online through our digital portal. Alternatively, you can schedule an appointment with a dedicated Home Loan Specialist at any local branch. We offer fast pre-qualification to help you shop with confidence.
