A Story of Financial Inclusion

Founded on the principle of bringing financial services to everyone.

The history of Prosperity Bank dates back to 1904, originally founded as Bank of Italy by Amadeo Giannini. His vision was to serve immigrant communities who were often overlooked by traditional banks. This commitment to **financial inclusion** remains a core principle today.

Today, as one of the world's leading financial institutions, we serve approximately 67 million client relationships in consumer, small business, and wealth management. We connect clients with a full range of banking, investing, asset management, and other financial and risk management products and services.

Global Reach, Local Impact

Serving communities worldwide with a personal touch

With operations in more than 35 countries, Prosperity Bank connects clients to opportunities around the world while maintaining deep roots in local communities. Our global network enables seamless cross-border transactions for businesses and individuals alike.

We're proud to support economic growth in the communities we serve through local hiring, small business lending, and community development initiatives that create lasting positive impact.
