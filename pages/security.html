Protect Yourself: Recognizing Scams

Knowledge is your best defense. Be aware of common fraud tactics used by criminals.

Phishing Emails

Never click on links or provide personal information in emails requesting account verification, especially if they look suspicious.

Vishing (Phone Scams)

Be wary of callers claiming to be from the bank asking for your one-time passcodes, PINs, or account numbers. We won't ask for this.

Wire Transfer Fraud

Do not wire funds to anyone you don't know, especially if they are pressuring you or asking you to pay a debt immediately.
